About

We’re working with refugee-related NGOs to reimagine volunteering. Basically, is it possible for people to contribute in small ways that don’t take a lot of their time. We’ve talked to the 3 primary NGOs in San Jose, the international rescue committee, catholic charities, and jewish family services and came up with an initial set of “tasks” that people can do to help out.
